Teacup (Unofficial Type)

Features:

  • Tiny size
  • Short-haired or long-haired

The teacup Chihuahua is recognizable due to its extremely small size. As an adult they could weigh around five pounds or less. Though with its small size comes a shorter lifespan and serious health issues, such as hydrocephalus, heart disease, bone fractures, and more.
