Apple Head (Unofficial Type)

Features:

  • Dome, "apple-shaped" head
  • Short snout
  • Large protruding eyes
  • Short jaw
  • Moleras

Apple head Chihuahuas have very distinct physical features. According to the American Kennel Club, the Chihuahua is supposed to have a well rounded "apple dome" skull with our without a molera (a hole on top of dogs skull). The shape of their head causes the eyes appear to protrude and they have short snouts. Apple head Chihuahuas are smaller in size than deer head Chihuahuas, as they have an average height of five to nine inches and a weight of two to six pounds.
