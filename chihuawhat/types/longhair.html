Long-Haired (Official Type)

Features:

  • Long fur
  • Fluffier look

Long-haired Chihuahuas are similar in all things in comparison to short-haired Chihuahuas, the mere difference is that they tend to have longer fur an a fluffier look. Due to their longer hair, this type requires more maintanence in terms of groooming.
