Fawn (Unofficial Type)

Features:

  • Fawn-colored
  • Short-haired or long-haired

The fawn Chihuahua is normal in height and size of the average Chihuahua, they are merely in fawn color. Fawn color is rather common amongst Chihuahuas and they come in many varieties such as blue fawn or sable fawn.
